Buyer funds secured before transfer. Buyer deposits full agreed consideration into escrow before domain transfer is initiated.

Controlled domain transfer. Domain transferred via registrar push or registrar transfer upon escrow confirmation of secured funds.
05
Buyer confirmation. Buyer confirms successful receipt and control of the domain before escrow releases funds.
06
Seller payout after successful completion. Funds released to seller only after buyer confirmation. Closing records archived.
05 — Why Qualification Is Required

Why Qualification Is Required

GigaSaudi.com is not a commodity. It is a category name.

A category name transferred to the wrong acquirer is a category name destroyed. A category name in the hands of the right acquirer becomes infrastructure.

The qualification requirement exists to protect the strategic integrity of the asset — for the acquirer as much as for the category itself.

A name that names Saudi Arabia’s giga-scale era at the highest level of abstraction carries a weight that most domain transactions do not. The qualification process is how that weight is acknowledged at the point of transfer.
