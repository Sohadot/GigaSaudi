01 — What Is Available

What Is Available

GigaSaudi.com is a .com naming coordinate at the intersection of the two most strategically resonant terms in Saudi Arabia's transformation era: "Giga" — the language of scale — and "Saudi" — the sovereign anchor of that transformation.

This is the naming layer. Not one project. Not one sector. The name for the scale above the projects.

The asset includes the domain, this public reference infrastructure, and the strategic positioning developed to establish the name as a category anchor.

It is available for transfer to a single qualified acquirer capable of developing it at the scale the category deserves.

02 — Qualified Acquirer Profile

Qualified Acquirer Profile

Not every interested party is a qualified acquirer. The name's strategic value depends on it being developed, protected, and deployed with intent.

01
Strategic relevance. The acquirer operates within, serves, or communicates about Saudi Arabia's giga-scale transformation era across capital, cities, energy, technology, tourism, sport, culture, logistics, or infrastructure.
02
Capacity to develop. The acquirer has the resources and organisational capability to deploy the asset at institutional scale — not to hold it dormant or redirect it to a lower-value purpose.
03
Ability to protect. The acquirer can maintain and defend the naming asset with legal, technical, and brand infrastructure appropriate to its category value.
04
Clear use case. The acquirer has a defined and credible deployment context: a platform, a brand, an editorial layer, a communications infrastructure, an intelligence product, or an institutional asset.
05
Intended deployment. The acquirer intends to activate the asset — not to park, resell, or warehouse it at a lower-value holding level than its strategic category warrants.

04 — Why Qualification Is Required

Why Qualification Is Required

GigaSaudi.com is not a commodity. It is a category name.

A category name transferred to the wrong acquirer is a category name destroyed. A category name in the hands of the right acquirer becomes infrastructure.

The qualification requirement exists to protect the strategic integrity of the asset — for the acquirer as much as for the category itself.

A name that names Saudi Arabia's giga-scale era at the highest level of abstraction carries a weight that most domain transactions do not. The qualification process is how that weight is acknowledged at the point of transfer.
